GOLD

Gold is a precious metal widely traded in financial markets. It holds intrinsic value and is often used as a store of wealth. It's used for jewelry, as well as in various industries like electronics and dentistry. In financial markets, gold is bought and sold as a commodity and as an investment. Its price is influenced by factors like economic stability, inflation rates, and geopolitical events. Many investors consider gold a hedge against inflation and economic uncertainties. Gold prices can be volatile but are generally seen as a stable long-term investment.
