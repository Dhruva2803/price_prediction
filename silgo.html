SILVER

Silver is a precious metal like gold but is more widely used in industrial applications due to its electrical conductivity and other properties. It's used in electronics, photography, and various industries. Like gold, silver is traded as a commodity and investment. It's also used for making jewelry and silverware. The price of silver is influenced by factors like industrial demand, economic conditions, and currency strength. While it can be volatile, silver is considered both a store of value and an industrial commodity. It's often seen as a more affordable alternative to gold for investors.
